Types of Inheritance :

1> Single Inheritance : one parent one child 
2> Multilevel Inheritance : one Parent child/Parent, child   GF - F - S
3> Multiple Inheritance : multiple Parent one Child (Not Allowed)
4> Hierarchical Inheritance : one parent multiple child
5> Hybrid Inheritance : mix of All
